The Evaluation Council

Six models. Six perspectives. Zero groupthink.

Composite fitness = base score − flaw penalty + rank adjustment + practitioner bonus, with confidence derived from cross-provider agreement. When confidence is low, more survivors are kept. When it's high, aggressive pruning selects only the top 3.

Quality Scorer

All 10 teams

Blind dimensional scoring against task-adaptive rubric. Custom dimensions and weights generated per task.

Adversarial Critic

Top 5

Finds contradictions, unsupported claims, missing elements, feasibility issues, and redundancy. Flaw counts penalize fitness.

Comparator

Top 5

Side-by-side relative ranking with shuffled, anonymized labels. Cites actual content differences, not vague quality claims.

Practitioner

Top 5

“Could you implement this tomorrow?” Tests for actionability, specificity, constraint acknowledgment. Separate model provider.

Cross-Check

Top 3

Same scoring dimensions, different provider. Cross-provider agreement drives confidence. Disagreement triggers conservative selection.

Compliance Auditor

All teams

Per-agent scope compliance, team coverage mapping, redundancy scoring. Verifies that role briefs are structurally enforced, not just suggested.
